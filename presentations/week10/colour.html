COLOUR PALETTES

MONO-CHROMATIC

This layout with a mono-chromatic colour scheme sets a harmonious and sweet mood and ties all the colours together, especially since this colour scheme is all based on one colour's hues. The base colour is easily established and relates to the content.

ANALOGOUS

The colour blue adds a mood of calm and coolness, while the purple enhances that and gives the scheme depth. It almost makes it feel playful and friendly. The chosen colour scheme helps the layout and content flow nicely and seamlessly.

COMPLEMENTARY

This layout has colours that compliment each other, hence the name of the colour scheme: complementary. The colours are bold and distinguishable from each other, and help balance out the layout.
