Logo Inspiration

How the logo shows what the company is about:

It represents the beauty and richness of our cocoa, which is reflected in our rich chocolate. The Comic Sans font brings out the playfulness in the products and helps it target younger demographics.

Why the logo is effective for the brand:

The parrot of the logo symbolizes the tropics and the geographic origins of our fair trade cocoa.

How the branding/logo makes us stand out:

It has bold colours and has a unique logo (a parrot symbol is unique in the chocolate industry), which helps it stand out from our competitors.
