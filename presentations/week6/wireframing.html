Using a wireframe is very helpful because it is an essential step in creating a website or doing any other type of project. It is after the brainstorming stage, and wireframing is essentially the planning stage of where the content will be placed. Even if there is no decided content, you can still create a wireframe to help organize the layout and the wireframe can help in figuring out how much content you want and need. My wireframe is very simple and minimalistic, and everything is laid out clearly to avoid confusion. Since the wireframe is for a car dealer's business, the main goal is to provide information about and effeciently sell the cars. The layout has a section/space for each set of content, such as the text, images, and links.
